The Vesperium’s Collections: A Journey Through Filipino Heritage

The Vesperium’s collections are an invitation to explore—a celebration of Filipino heritage in all its forms, from the tools of ancient craftsmen to the weapons of revolutionaries. Here, an intricately carved Kampilan sword holds as much significance as the grand portraits of national heroes. Every artifact tells a story, bridging the past and present through the ingenuity, resilience, and artistry of the Filipino people.

By contributing to the Vesperium, you become a vital part of our mission. Whether through donations, volunteering, or sponsorship, your support helps us curate new exhibitions, restore invaluable artifacts, and provide scholarships for aspiring scholars. Join us in safeguarding our nation’s past while shaping its future—every contribution makes a lasting impact.
